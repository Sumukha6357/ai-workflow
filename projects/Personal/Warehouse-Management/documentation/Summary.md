# Project Summary: Warehouse-Management

## 📦 Overview
Warehouse-Management (Logistics Ledger) is an enterprise-grade, full-stack monorepo designed for high-performance warehouse operations. It focuses on real-time inventory tracking, staff management, and operational telemetrics.

## 🎯 Primary Objectives
- **Inventory Precision**: Real-time tracking of stock levels and movements.
- **Operational Speed**: Intelligent assignment of pick/pack tasks via WebSockets.
- **Security**: Advanced RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) to prevent unauthorized stock tampering.
- **Observability**: Live telemetrics for management to monitor warehouse floor throughput.

## 🚀 Key Value Proposition
The system bridges the gap between traditional WMS (Warehouse Management Systems) and modern real-time data streaming, providing a "live" ledger of every physical moving part in the logistics chain.
